Pictlist

fast and colorful lists on your iphone or ipad

Lists with pictures - what a novel idea!

I don't know about you but I find most List creation apps - well - kind of boring. I wanted something with a little color to make it easier for me to easily and quickly pick out one list from another. The same goes for when I'm working with a list, for example my grocery list. I wanted to be able to see what was in my list with less fuss and more fun.

The other problem I had with the list apps I tried is how much of a pain it could be to create and edit lists. I wanted something that was fast and easy. More importantly I wanted my app to remember the things I used in my lists so I could re-use them in new lists.

So - I created Picklist (get it: lists with pictures ;-).
